# 10 Must-Have Accessories for Your Tesla Model Y Juniper

Just picked up your Model Y Juniper? Congrats — it's a hell of a car.

But here's the thing: Tesla ships a great vehicle with a few obvious gaps. No all-weather mats. No screen protection. A center console that's basically a bottomless pit.

I've spent weeks researching, reading hundreds of Reddit threads, watching YouTube reviews, and testing products in my own Juniper. Here are the 10 accessories that are actually worth your money — no filler, no junk.

---

## 1. All-Weather Floor Mats — 3D MAXpider Kagu

**Why it's #1:** Your Juniper's carpet will get destroyed without protection. This is the single most important accessory you can buy.

The 3D MAXpider Kagu mats are the gold standard. Three-layer construction with a patented fiber backing that grips without damaging your carpet. Raised edges catch every drop of water, mud, and coffee you'll inevitably spill.

**Why not WeatherTech?** WeatherTech is solid, but the MAXpider looks more premium and fits just as well — most Reddit owners who've tried both prefer the Kagu.

- **Price:** ~$70-230 (depending on full set vs individual)
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=3D+MAXpider+Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+2026&tag=teslamodelguy-20) | [EV Bandit](https://evbandit.com/collections/3d-maxpider?sca_ref=10733634.pROAnZ9F8x)

**Runner-up:** Tesmanian mats — about 30% cheaper, still great coverage with velcro-like strips to stay in place.

## 2. Screen Protector — Spigen EZ Fit Tempered Glass

**Why you need it immediately:** The Juniper has a gorgeous 15.4-inch touchscreen. It will get scratched. It will get covered in fingerprints. The matte version kills glare on sunny days.

Spigen's EZ Fit comes with an alignment tray that makes installation foolproof — no bubbles, perfectly centered every time. 9H hardness, oleophobic coating, maintains full touch responsiveness.

⚠️ **Important:** The Juniper screen is 15.4", not 15" like the old Model Y. Old screen protectors DO NOT FIT. Make sure you buy one that says "Juniper" or "2025-2026."

- **Price:** ~$30-40
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Spigen+Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+screen+protector&tag=teslamodelguy-20)

**Runner-up:** dbrand Prism 2.0 — premium option with the best self-aligning install tray in the business, but costs more.

## 3. Center Console Organizer

**Why you need it:** The Juniper redesigned the center console, and without an organizer, small items just disappear into a black hole. Keys, sunglasses, charging cables — gone.

Look for one with multiple compartments and a rubber/silicone lining so nothing rattles while you drive.

- **Price:** ~$20-35
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+center+console+organizer&tag=teslamodelguy-20) | [EVBASE](https://www.evbase.com/products/model-y-juniper-model-3-highland-center-console-organizer-storage-box-with-usb-charging-port-for-tesla)

**Pro tip:** EVBASE makes one with a built-in USB charging port — worth the extra $5.

## 4. Roof Sunshade

**Why you need it:** The Juniper's glass roof is beautiful. It also turns your car into a greenhouse in summer. A sunshade drops cabin temp by 10-15°F and reduces AC load.

EVBASE makes a Juniper-specific sunshade with improved heat rejection designed to fit the exact dimensions of the refreshed roof.

- **Price:** ~$50-80
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+sunshade+roof&tag=teslamodelguy-20)

## 5. Mud Flaps

**Why you need them:** Tesla's paint is famously thin. Without mud flaps, road debris will chip your rear quarter panels within months. This is $20 insurance against hundreds in paint repair.

No-drill options are available — they clip directly onto existing mounting points.

- **Price:** ~$20-30
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+mud+flaps&tag=teslamodelguy-20)

---

## 6. SSD for Sentry Mode & Dashcam

**Why you need it:** Tesla's Sentry Mode and built-in dashcam need external storage. A USB flash drive works but is slow and unreliable. A Samsung T7 SSD is the move — fast, reliable, and small enough to tuck away.

- **Price:** ~$60-80 (500GB)
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon — Samsung T7](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Samsung+T7+SSD+500GB&tag=teslamodelguy-20)

**Pro tip:** Format it as exFAT before plugging in. Tesla won't recognize NTFS or APFS.

---

**Why it matters:** The Juniper added an 8-inch rear passenger screen — a first for Model Y. If you have kids, this thing will get poked, smudged, and abused. Protect it from day one.

Spigen makes a matching rear screen protector that uses the same EZ Fit system.

- **Price:** ~$15-20
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Tesla+Model+Y+Juniper+rear+screen+protector&tag=teslamodelguy-20)

**Why you need it:** The Model Y trunk is massive but has zero organization. Groceries slide everywhere. A collapsible trunk organizer with dividers keeps everything in place and folds flat when not needed.

## 10. Portable Tire Inflator

**Why it's essential:** The Model Y doesn't come with a spare tire. If you get a flat on the highway, you need a portable inflator and repair kit. This isn't optional — it's safety equipment.

The AstroAI or Fanttik X8 are compact enough to live in your sub-trunk permanently.

- **Price:** ~$30-50
- **Where to buy:** [Amazon — Portable Tire Inflator](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=portable+tire+inflator+car&tag=teslamodelguy-20)
