## 📊 Platform-Specific Strategy

### Reddit (r/TeslaModelY, r/ModelY, r/electricvehicles)
**Hot topics:**
- Delivery inspection anxiety (panel gaps, paint)
- "What accessories did you get?" threads
- Suspension complaints → opportunity for ride improvement accessories
- Winter range anxiety → winter tips content
- FSD experiences
**Strategy:** Answer questions naturally, link to our guides when relevant. Don't spam.

### Pinterest
**Hot pin formats:**
- Before/after accessory installs
- "10 Must-Have" vertical infographics
- Product comparison side-by-side images
- Interior organization hacks
**Strategy:** Create 5 pins per article. Vertical format (1000x1500px). Rich descriptions with keywords.

### YouTube Shorts
**Hot topics:**
- 60-second accessory reviews
- "Is this worth it?" product tests
- Before/after installs
- Hidden features quick tips
**Strategy:** Repurpose article content into 15-60 second clips. Link to full article in description.

## 🎯 SEO Best Practices Applied

Based on Google Helpful Content Update 2025-2026:

1. **E-E-A-T First:** Every article mentions real ownership, real experience
2. **FAQ Sections:** Each article has structured FAQ for featured snippets
3. **Internal Linking:** Every article links to 2-3 related articles
4. **Affiliate Disclosure:** Transparent, prominently placed
5. **Juniper-Specific:** Always mention "Juniper" compatibility to capture refresh-specific searches
6. **Update Dates:** All articles show "Last updated" — signals freshness to Google
7. **Long-Form Depth:** 1500-3000 words per review, not thin affiliate listicles
8. **Comparison Tables:** Google loves structured data for shopping queries
9. **Original Insights:** Include Reddit consensus, community sentiment — not just product specs
